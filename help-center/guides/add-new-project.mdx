---

## Tutorial

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://alysidi.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Click on "Dashboard" in the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Click "Create New Project".
    Give your project a name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Add team members by email if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

All set! Your new project now appears in your Aly Sidi dashboard.
