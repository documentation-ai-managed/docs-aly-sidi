## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for sign-up
- Your audience data ready (CSV file or manual entry)
- Basic knowledge of email marketing best practices
</Callout>

## Sign Up for Your Free Trial

Start by creating your TinyEmail account. The process takes less than 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Go to the [TinyEmail sign-up page](https://app.tinyemail.com/sign-up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Provide your email, name, and password. Verify your email address via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Answer a few quick questions about your business to customize your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You now have access to the TinyEmail dashboard at [https://app.tinyemail.com](https://app.tinyemail.com).
</Callout>

## Add Your First Audience List

Build your subscriber list to start sending emails.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Audience" icon="users">
    Click `Audience` in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New List">
    Select `+ New List` and name it (e.g., "Newsletter Subscribers").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Subscribers">
    Upload a CSV file or add manually.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here's a sample CSV format for import:

````csv
email,name,signup_date
user@example.com,John Doe,2024-01-15
jane@sample.com,Jane Smith,2024-01-16
````

## Create and Send Your First Campaign

Launch a simple welcome email.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag-and-Drop Editor" icon="mouse-pointer">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start New Campaign" icon="plus">
        Go to `Campaigns` > `+ New Campaign`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Template">
        Choose a `Welcome` template.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize Content">
        Edit subject: `Welcome to TinyEmail!`
        
        Add your message:
        
````html
<h1>Hello!</h1>
<p>Thanks for joining. Start growing your audience today.</p>
<p><a href="{UNSUBSCRIBE_LINK}">Unsubscribe</a></p>
````
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Audience & Send">
        Choose your list, review, and click `Send`.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="HTML Editor" icon="code">
    Use custom HTML for advanced designs.
    
````html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<body>
  <h1>Welcome aboard!</h1>
  <p>Track opens and clicks in Analytics.</p>
</body>
</html>
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
